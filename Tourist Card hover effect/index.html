Pyramids of Giza, three 4th-dynasty (c. 2575–c. 2465 BCE) pyramids erected on a rocky plateau on the west bank of the Nile River near Al-Jīzah (Giza) in northern Egypt.

The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts centre in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.
